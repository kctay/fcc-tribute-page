Zinedine Zidane

The Football Legend

Zinedine Zidane with the 2018 UEFA Champions League Trophy
Here's a time line of Zinedine Zidane's career:
  • 1972 - Born in Marseille, France
  • 1981 - Started Youth career with US Saint-Henri
  • 1989 - Started Senior career with AS Cannes
  • 1992 - Transferred to Bordeaux in the 92/93 season
  • 1994 - Won first cap with France, coming on as a substitute against Czech Republic
  • 1996 - Moved to Juventus
  • 1997 - Helped Juventus win the Seria A title
  • 1998 - Won 97/98 Seria A title. Won the 1998 World Cup. Named FIFA World Player of the Year, and won the Ballon d'Or
  • 2000 - Won Euro 2000. named Player of the Tournament by UEFA. Won FIFA World Player of the Year
  • 2001 - Signed for Real Madrid for a world record fee of €77.5 million
  • 2002 - Helped Madrid to a 2–1 win over Bayer Leverkusen in the 2002 UEFA Champions League Final, scoring a stunning goal
  • 2003 - Helped Madrid win the 2002–03 La Liga. Named FIFA World Player of the Year for the third time
  • 2006 - Retired after the 2006 World Cup

"Zidane is the master. Over the past ten years, there's been no one like him, he has been the best player in the world."
